# Learn Assembly (x86/x86_64)

Welcome to your first course to learn assembler for **x86 processors**, in this course we will see the basics of assembler as well as a little history on this language if you want to change subject you will have an index containing all the chapters, at the end of this training you will be able to develop in assembler, to better understand your system can be even create an operating system, or a disassembler, contrary to what we can think the assembler is still very used in computer science in particular in the embedded development or the reverse engineering and offers a thorough knowledge with good solid bases in the world of programming.
